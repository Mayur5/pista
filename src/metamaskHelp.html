Steps to install MetaMask

Part 1 - Installing MetaMask

Step 1

Click here to go to the Chrome Webstore and add the MetaMask plugin.

Step 2

Click "Add to Chrome" to Install MetaMask as Google Chrome extension.

Step 3

Click "Add Extension" to confirm and MetaMask will be added

You can see that MetaMask is added by the little fox logo that shows up on the top right corner

Part 2 - Creating a new account

Step 1

Click the MetaMask logo

Step 2

Read the privacy notice. Scroll down the Terms of Use and click ‘Accept’ (the “Accept” button will only activate if you scroll the contract all the way down).

Step 3

Fill in a strong password to use MetaMask and click Create. Make sure it’s secure!

Step 4

MetaMask will now give you a ‘seed phrase’ that we advise you to keep somewhere safe where you are sure to find it (and no one else).

Step 5

Congratulations! You now have your first Ethereum account.
